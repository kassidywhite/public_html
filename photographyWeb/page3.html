About Me


My name is Kassidy, but my friends know me as Kass! I am in fact not a photographer and never have even tried photography. To be frank, this portfolio is just a bunch of license-free pictures I compiled from pexels.

In a website like this, the photographer would typically talk about their passion for photography, however, since I am not a photographer, I will just talk about how much I love sushi.

Sushi is literally the best. If you say that you don't like sushi, you are lying to yourself. It's literally the perfect food. The ratio of rice to seafood is perfect. Take it from me because I literally don't even like seafood, but sushi is so good that all my predisposed hatred towards fish just disappears when I have sushi. Moral of the story is if you say you don't like it, I recommend you give it another try. #sushiforall
